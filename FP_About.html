Hello! My name is Megan. I have a Bachelors in 3D Animation. This is for my Web Publishing Technology class, CMT421. This is my Final Project. I chose to create a Choose Your Own Adventure story. The story is highly inspired by the 1977 game "Zork", developed by Infocom and the medium is inspired by the 2009-2017 Webcomic "Homestuck" written by Andrew Hussie.

This website was made through GoDaddy, Github, and Notepad++; and made for my final semester at EMU, Fall 2024.
