>> Go through the Doorway. <<

You open the door and peer inside.

You see a vending machine! Trotting up to it, you feel your pockets, hoping to find any kind of loose pocket change. You unfortunately find your pockets empty, and while inspecting the machine, you also find the buttons smashed in.

The light inside the machine still works. There’s condensation on the chilled soda bottles inside. Are you so thirsty that you could maybe… tip.. the machine over?

Leave it Alone.

Let Gravity do its thing!
